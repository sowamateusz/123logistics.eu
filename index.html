About us

123Logistics.eu is a young, fast-growing shipping company based in Wroclaw, Poland. Our experienced team specializes in organizing and providing road transport solutions throughout the continent of Europe.

Focused on the needs of our costumers, we offer industry specific expertise for businesses in the industrial, automotive, healthcare, lifestyle and hi-tech sectors. In the process of transport organization, we implement innovative solutions such as online vehicle tracking, temperature control, and the notification system concerning the status of the cargo tailored accordingly to our costumers' desires and needs.

Road transport

We are experts in the field of express transport organization throughout Europe. The 123logistics.eu team specializes in the delivery chain service for the pharmaceutical and automotive lines of businesses. Therefore, you can be sure that the trade goods will be delivered to the contracting party accordingly to the expectations. Our expanded, well-matched haulier base enables us to execute any freight, no matter the means of transport, route and date.
